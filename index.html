HELLO WORLD WELCOME 2 MY EDWARD CULLEN FAN PAGE!!1!1!!!!!

Diary Entry 1: February 14, 2008 Dear Diary, Today is Valentine's Day and I can't stop thinking about Edward Cullen. He is so dreamy and perfect. I wrote a poem about him: Edward, oh Edward, with skin so pale, Your love for me will never fail. Your eyes so golden, like the sun, I wish our love had just begun. I wish Edward was my Valentine. Maybe one day he will notice me. Love, mrs edward cullen

Diary Entry 2: March 10, 2008 Dear Diary, I saw the movie Twilight again today. Edward is so romantic and handsome. I wish I could meet someone like him in real life. I wrote another poem: Edward, my love, so strong and true, I wish I could be with you. Your vampire charm, your dazzling smile, Makes my heart race a mile. I can't wait for the next movie to come out. I hope Edward and I stay together forever. Love, mrs edward cullen

Diary Entry 3: April 23, 2008 Dear Diary, I found out that Robert Pattinson, the actor who plays Edward, is going to be in a new movie. I'm so excited! I wrote a poem to celebrate: Robert, oh Robert, with hair so wild, Your talent as an actor is truly styled. As Edward Cullen, you stole my heart, In every scene, you played your part. I can't wait to see Robert in his new movie. I know he will be amazing. Love, mrsedwardcullen

Diary Entry 4: May 30, 2008 Dear Diary, I just finished reading Twilight for the third time. I love the part where Edward saves Bella from the car accident. He is so brave and heroic. I wrote a poem about it: Edward, my hero, so strong and true, I wish I could be saved by you. Your love for Bella, so deep and pure, In my heart, it will always endure. I wish I could live in the world of Twilight forever. Edward is the perfect boyfriend. Love, MRSEDWARDCULLEN

Diary Entry 5: June 25, 2008 Dear Diary, Today is my birthday and all I want is for Edward Cullen to be real. I know it's stupid, but I can't help dreaming about him. I wrote one last poem: Edward, my love, in books you dwell, In my heart, you cast a spell. I wish I could be by your side, In Forks, where our love could abide. I hope one day I can find you and we can be vampires together forever!!!!!!. Love, MRSEDWARDCULLEN
